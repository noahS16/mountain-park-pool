What's Included

Full pool access every day of the season, Mon–Sun from 6am to 12am. Your membership covers your entire household (up to 4) + 6 guests — no per-visit fees.

Members are allowed to bring up to 6 guest - no additional fees. There is a strict 10 people max per visit. Anything over 10 will be considered a party - please use our booking system to reserve a date.

Charcoal grills will be available inside the park on a first come first serve basis throughout the season. Please be mindful and share the grills with other members. The roundhouse includes 2 restrooms and a ping pong table. Please bring your own ball and paddles.

Members receive 30% off private pool rentals for birthdays, reunions, and other events. Contact us to check availability and book.

Eligible Area

Membership is open to residents in the surrounding Northeast El Paso neighborhoods. Don't live in the area? Contact us for availability.
