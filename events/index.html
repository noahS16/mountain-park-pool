How it works

The top area of the park will be reserved for your event. Grills will be on a first come first serve basis. We do NOT close the park. Other members will still be allowed to enter and swim. We may require you to provide a lifeguard and/or security depending on the size of the event.

You and your party will have access to the roundhouse, which includes bathrooms and a ping pong table. Please bring your own ball and paddles.

Grills will be available to use, but are also still available to other members. You are welcome to bring your own grill.

After filling out the booking form, we will contact you ASAP to confirm your date.
