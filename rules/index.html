Pool Rules

Please read and follow all guidelines to keep Mountain Park Pool safe and enjoyable for everyone.

Remember to check in on every visit

Scan the QR code at the pool gate or use the link below to log your visit.

Access & Entry

A gate code is required to enter the pool. This code is provided to active members only and is non-transferable. Do not share your code with non-members.

Pool hours are 6am – 12am daily, May 25 – September 7. No entry before or after hours.

Only registered household members may enter. Guests must be accompanied by a member at all times.

A person 16 years or older must be present if children are in the group.

Groups of 10 or more people are considered a private event and may be subject to an event fee. Please book in advance.

Safety

No running on the pool deck or surrounding areas.

No diving into the pool.

No roughhousing, pushing, dunking, or aggressive play.

In case of emergency, call 911 first, then contact pool management.

Pool Conduct

Bluetooth speakers are welcome — keep volume respectful of neighbors.

Alcohol is permitted for adults 21+. Please drink responsibly.

Absolutely no glass of any kind on the premises.

No food inside the pool.

You are welcome to bring your own pool toys and floaties.

Facilities

Grills are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Please clean up after use.

Leave the pool and surrounding area clean when you leave. Dispose of all trash properly.

If you are the last to leave, please turn off all lights and ensure the gate is locked behind you.

Membership

Membership is valid for the current season only and is non-refundable.

Mountain Park Pool management reserves the right to revoke membership for repeated or serious rule violations without refund.

By using Mountain Park Pool you agree to follow these rules and our membership terms. Questions? Call us at (915) 861-5336.
